Welcome to Bridger

Bridger was created to give virtual Students, Alumni, and Staff at FHSU a platform to find and network with each other. The creator of Bridger found it difficult to find other students local to him to create professional connections.

To use Bridger you need a Facebook account to log-on to our site. You can log-in at the bottom of every page. Once logged in you will be able to access your profile page and view the map of other user's locations.
