Frequently Asked Questions

Why create Bridger?
Bridger was created to allow virtual students a way of connecting with other local virtual students and alumni. Allowing them to network and further their career.
Why do I need to log into Facebook?
Instead of recreating the wheel and making a login, Facebook API was used to create a secure login.
Why can't I connect with anyone?
Bridger is still under construction, please bear with us while we put the finnishing touches on our site.
How can I let you know of problems or suggestions?
Go to the "Contact Us" page and shoot us an email.
Can anyone sign up?
The site was made with virtual students in mind, but nothing is stopping someone who goes to campus to join.
