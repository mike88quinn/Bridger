Bridge Connections

Bridger was created to allow Students, Alumni, and Staff of FHSU to find eachother. The purpose of finding eachother is to increase networking for virtual students. Students still in school can find eachother to meet up and work on classes. Students can also make connections with Alumni to help further or jumpstart their careers.
